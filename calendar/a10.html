Araw ng Kagitingan

Date: April 10 (Mon)

Araw ng Kagitingan honors the courage and sacrifice of Filipino and American soldiers during World War II, particularly commemorating the fall of Bataan on April 9, 1942. The holiday pays tribute to the bravery shown during one of the darkest moments of Philippine history when troops fought fiercely despite overwhelming odds. The day is marked by ceremonies, wreath-laying events, and reflections on patriotism and sacrifice. It serves as a reminder of the resilience and heroism of those who fought for the country’s freedom.
